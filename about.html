About FlareSolverr

Who We Are

FlareSolverr is an open-source project dedicated to providing developers with powerful tools to overcome web scraping challenges. Our proxy server automatically solves Cloudflare and DDoS-GUARD protection, making it easier for developers to access the data they need.

Born from the frustration of dealing with anti-bot measures, FlareSolverr has grown into a trusted solution used by thousands of developers worldwide. We believe in the power of open-source collaboration and the importance of accessible, reliable tools for the developer community.
